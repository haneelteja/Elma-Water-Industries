## USAGE NOTES

- Send reorder reminders 7–10 days before the client is likely to run out (track their usual order cadence)
- Seasonal messages work best sent 2 weeks before the season starts
- Referral asks work best right after a smooth delivery when the client is happiest
- Keep a simple tracker: Client | Last Order Date | Next Expected Reorder | Notes
