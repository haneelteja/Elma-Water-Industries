## USAGE NOTES

- Always personalise: use the restaurant/business name
- Keep the opening line specific to their type of business
- Lead with social proof (45+ brands, named clients) whenever possible
- Always end with a clear, low-friction CTA (sample, call, quote)
- Don't send Follow-up 2 unless Follow-up 1 also went unanswered
