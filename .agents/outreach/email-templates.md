## USAGE NOTES

- Always personalise [First Name] and [Restaurant/Business Name] — generic blasts get ignored
- E1 and E2 are interchangeable for restaurants — test both subject lines
- For hotels: always emphasise the hospitality angle (in-room, banquet, events)
- For cloud kitchens: emphasise delivery packaging and brand recall
- Send follow-ups in the same thread (reply to original) — keeps context
- Best send times: Tue–Thu, 10–11 AM or 3–4 PM
- Track replies in a simple spreadsheet: Name | Business | Date sent | Status
