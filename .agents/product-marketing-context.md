## 3. Personas

### The Restaurant Owner / Manager (Primary B2B)

- Runs a restaurant or food brand in Hyderabad
- Currently using generic water bottles — brand opportunity being wasted
- Cares about: consistent supply, on-time delivery, label print quality, competitive pricing per case
- Decision trigger: Opening new outlet, rebranding, existing supplier failing them
- Already familiar with the concept — needs a reliable, quality vendor

### The Food Brand / FMCG Buyer

- Marketing or ops manager at a food brand
- Wants branded water for retail, events, or corporate gifting
- Cares about: label design quality, food-safe certifications, MOQ flexibility

### The Event/Wedding Planner (Individual Custom)

- Ordering personalised bottles for a specific occasion
- Smaller quantities, higher design customisation
- Driven by aesthetics and personalisation

## 4. Problems & Pain Points

**Core B2B challenge:**
Restaurants in Hyderabad serve generic, unbranded water — a missed opportunity to reinforce their brand at every table.

### B2B operational pain

- Existing suppliers are unreliable — late deliveries, inconsistent label quality
- No single vendor offering both good quality AND reliable service in the local market
- Managing label design approvals and reorders is cumbersome

### Emotional tension

- "Our food brand is premium — why are we serving water in a no-name bottle?"
- "I've been burned by suppliers who promise quality but can't deliver consistently"

### Individual custom pain

- Hard to find local vendors who do small custom batches at reasonable prices
- Design-to-delivery experience is usually slow or low quality

## 5. Competitive Landscape

### Direct competitors (Hyderabad)

- Local PET bottle manufacturers/suppliers who offer basic labelling
- Generic private-label water brands restaurants currently use

### Indirect competitors

- Restaurants making do with unlabelled or generically labelled bottles
- National branded water (Bisleri, Kinley, Aquafina) used as table water

### Elma's edge

- Superior service, delivery reliability, and product quality vs. existing market options
- Custom label quality that matches brand aesthetics
- Local presence = faster turnaround than non-Hyderabad vendors
- Relationship-driven business with 45 clients already validating the model

## 7. Objections & Anti-Personas

### Top objections from prospects

1. "We already have a supplier" → Counter: show the quality and service difference; offer a trial case
2. "Is the label print food-safe / durable?" → Address with quality specs and existing client proof
3. "What's your minimum order?" → Be clear on MOQ for bulk vs. individual

### Poor-fit customers

- Businesses wanting rock-bottom commodity pricing with no brand care
- Clients outside Hyderabad delivery radius (for now)
